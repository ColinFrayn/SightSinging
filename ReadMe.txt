1. Installation
---------------

Just unzip the contents somewhere suitable on your PC.
To run, double-click "SightSinging.exe"

2. Usage
--------

Choose a voice part and difficulty from the Settings drop down menu. 
Hit "Generate" to generate a random sequence of notes.
You can use the "Cue" button to give you your first note, and the Play/Stop, >, < buttons to control playback.
You can adjust playback speed and volume with the two slider controls.
